Learn more about me. Let me introduce myself.

Hello! My name is Leandro. I live in Germany, but come from Brazil (could you guess from the name?).

I am a Computer Engineer with an MBA from Mannheim Business School. My GitHub account is still young, and here you'll find out why!

All my ideas in just one place. There are just too many social networks!

That's why using this site to share all my most important thoughts, perceptions and ideas will help me spread my ideas in a much richer way

As you can see, this site is still in it's infancy. I have lots of ideas, and very little time.

For now, have a look into the one-page app I started to build. It uses the fetch Web API to get some data from a server, JavaScript to generate the html tags. There's also a bit of css, but that's not really the focus.

There's always work to do! And I love getting things done!

I am i currently learning A LOT about React and web development. Writting thousands of lines of code, to get Fintta's new app and website runing ASAP!

Besides thas technical part, I'm also product owner/ scrum master, I paying close attention to the organizational and project management aspects of the development. The team is still small, but starting off with best practices is always better than changing them with the team up and running. (We're agile, but we're also human).

From many alternatives, I chose a hybrid Scrumban approach. I believe that by themselves, Scrum sprints doesn't adapt fast enough, and Kanban's continuous delivery loop fails to release that dopaminergic effect of reaching a goal, and starting anew. Together, they are stronger.
